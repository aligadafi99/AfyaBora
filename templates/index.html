Cardiology

Cardiology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the study and treatment of disorders related to the heart and the circulatory system.

Cardiologists are medical professionals who specialize in the diagnosis and management of conditions such as heart disease, heart failure, coronary artery disease, valvular heart diseases, and various other cardiovascular disorders.
